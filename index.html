Top three activities to do at Nairobi

Attend a show or a concert

The Nairobi National Theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a game drive in the local game parks

The Nairobi national park is perfect for seeing some wild animal including the big five.

Play sports at the National stadium

Play volleyball, football, basketball or lift some weights.
